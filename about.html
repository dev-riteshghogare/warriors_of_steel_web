About Warriors of Steel

Warriors of Steel is a thrilling 2D fighting game developed by DuoHunters. We aimed to create a fast-paced, visually stunning fighting experience with a diverse roster of characters and a compelling storyline. Our passion for fighting games and dedication to quality drove us to create a game we're proud to share with you.
